Meet Andrea

Originally from Colombia Andrea keeps the world close to her heart, being socially and environmentally conscious. She loves animals especially dogs and hopes to soon explore life in Kenya through her camera lens. She enjoys the simpler things in life like spending time with family and friends over some new foods.

Meet Vicky

With one foot in Europe and the other in Africa Vicky is a global citizen. She loves exploring faraway places with exotic foods and cultures, but always loves to come home to London. She is next planning to travel China in the footsteps of Marco Polo. She loves lazy weekends sleeping in and exploring food markets in local areas.
